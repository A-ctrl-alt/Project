Know what can and cannot be recycled in your area. Always check local guidelines!

Plastics (Code 1 & 2)

Bottles, jars, and rigid containers. Rinse thoroughly! Check for the number inside the triangle symbol.

Paper & Cardboard

Junk mail, magazines, cardboard boxes. Must be clean and dry. **Pizza boxes with grease are NOT recyclable.**

Glass

Beverage and food jars. Colors can usually be mixed, but some facilities separate them. Remove lids first.

Metals

Aluminum and steel cans. Empty and rinse. Crush them to save space if your local center allows.

üå≥ Composting & Decomposition

Reduce landfill waste by composting organic materials. Decomposition is nature's ultimate recycling process.

🥬

The Greens (Nitrogen)

Fruit/vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, grass clippings. This provides the protein for the compost pile.

🌲

The Browns (Carbon)

Dead leaves, shredded paper, wood chips, and sawdust. This provides the energy and bulk.

💧

Moisture & Air

Keep the pile as damp as a wrung-out sponge, and turn it regularly to ensure proper aeration.
