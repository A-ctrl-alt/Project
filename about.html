Our Commitment to Green Living

Eco Tracker was founded in 2023 with a simple goal: to make sustainability accessible and engaging for everyone. We believe that small, consistent actions, when multiplied across a community, can lead to **monumental change**.

Our Technology

Our application uses real-time data and scientific models to give you an accurate picture of your ecological footprint. We translate complex environmental science into actionable, easy-to-digest metrics, helping you understand *how* and *where* to make the biggest difference.

Join the Movement

We are a community-driven platform. We encourage feedback, suggestions for new features, and sharing your success stories as we collectively work towards a healthier planet.
